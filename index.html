Team

Team RIO2 consists of 12 members all from the Diocesan Boys’ School Robotics Team.Our members range from Grade 8-12, all aspired to build a future of robotics through joining the FTC to strive for this goal. Even though 6 members of our team are new to the FTC competition, they are prepared to go out of their comfort zone to gain knowledge and prowess in the realm of robotics. Not only do these fellow teammates have a passion for robotics and engineering, they also have the skills and talent to pursue these passions to the highest degree. Our team name is called RIO2 (Our team name last year was “RIO”) as not only is Rio (A bird in a movie) the nickname of our team mascot, it also symbolizes courage, friendship and also teamwork, and we do believe that these values reflect the nature of our team. Moreover, it also represents the continuation of experience and the process of learning through mistakes and failures in the previous season.

About

Description of the society

We are an enthusiastic team of young dreamers, aiming to spread our passion and love for robotics. Our members engage in various activities such as classes, workshops and competitions to further develop their knowledge and understanding of robotics and programming. We also hold competitions to challenge our members and promote innovation. We believe in fostering a culture of creativity and collaboration in our club, and hope to make more legends out of the team.
